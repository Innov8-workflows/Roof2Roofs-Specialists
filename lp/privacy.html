Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

This policy explains how Roof 2 Roofs Specialist Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects and uses your personal information when you use this website or request a roof inspection or quote, and the rights you have under UK data protection law.

Who we are

Roof 2 Roofs Specialist Ltd is a roofing business covering Luton, High Wycombe, Reading, Oxford, Beaconsfield, Thame and the surrounding Thames Valley. We are the "data controller" responsible for your personal information.

You can contact us about your data, or to make any of the requests below, by phone or WhatsApp on 07523 038369, or by email at info@roof2roofsspecialistltd.co.uk.

The information we collect

Information you give us — when you complete the quote form, we collect the details you provide, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address (optional)
  • Your postcode / the property location
  • The type of work you need and how soon you need it

When you choose to send your enquiry via WhatsApp, that message is handled by WhatsApp under its own terms.

Information collected automatically (only with your consent) — if we enable analytics or advertising cookies in future, and you accept them, we may use tools such as Google Analytics and the Meta (Facebook) Pixel to understand how people find and use the site and how our advertising performs. This may include your approximate location, IP address, device and browser type, the pages you view and how you arrived at the site.

How and why we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry — to arrange your free inspection, prepare your quote and contact you about it. Our lawful basis is taking steps at your request before entering into a contract, and our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries.
  • To provide our services — to carry out the work and keep records of it. Our lawful basis is performance of our contract with you and compliance with our legal obligations.
  • To measure and improve the site and our advertising — if and when analytics/advertising cookies are used. Our lawful basis is your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

Who we share your information with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted providers who help us run our business and act on our instructions (for example, messaging platforms such as WhatsApp, and — if enabled — analytics and advertising providers such as Google and Meta Platforms). Some providers may process data outside the UK, with appropriate safeguards in place. We may also disclose information where required by law.

How long we keep your information

We keep your information only for as long as we need it to deal with your enquiry and provide our services, and then for a reasonable period afterwards for our business records and to meet legal or tax obligations (typically up to six years). After that it is securely deleted.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct information that is wrong or incomplete
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Ask us to restrict or object to how we use it
  • Ask for a copy of certain information in a portable format
  • Withdraw your consent at any time (for anything based on consent)

To exercise any of these, please contact us on 07523 038369. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you are unhappy with how we have handled your information.

Keeping your information secure

We take appropriate measures to keep your information safe and to prevent unauthorised access to or loss of it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
